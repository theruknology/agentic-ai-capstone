Job Title: Senior Bioinformatics Scientist
Location: Boston, MA
Type: Full-time

We are seeking a Bioinformatics Scientist to join our genomic research team. The ideal candidate will develop computational pipelines for analyzing Next-Generation Sequencing (NGS) data.

Responsibilities:
- Design statistical pipelines in R or Python to analyze RNA-seq and microarray data.
- Develop network visualization tools using Cytoscape.
- Collaborate with wet-lab biologists to interpret experimental results.

Requirements:
- MS or PhD in Computational Biology, Bioinformatics, or related field.
- Strong proficiency in Python, R, and SQL.
- Experience with NGS data analysis and tools like BLAST or CellNetAnalyser.
- Published research in genomic signal processing is a plus.
